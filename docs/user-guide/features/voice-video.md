## üéØ Pro Tips & Best Practices

### Optimizing Voice Quality

**Hardware Recommendations:**
- **Dedicated headset** over computer speakers
- **USB microphones** for better quality
- **Stable internet connection** (3+ Mbps for video)
- **Wired connection** preferred over Wi-Fi

**Environment Setup:**
- **Quiet space** reduces background noise
- **Soft furnishings** minimize echo
- **Proper microphone positioning** (2-3 inches from mouth)
- **Good lighting** for video calls

### Etiquette Guidelines

**Voice Chat Etiquette:**
- **Mute when not speaking** in large groups
- **Use push-to-talk** in noisy environments
- **Announce arrivals/departures** in active conversations
- **Respect speaking turns** and avoid interrupting

**Video Call Etiquette:**
- **Stable camera position** - avoid moving around
- **Professional background** for work calls
- **Eye contact** - look at camera, not screen
- **Appropriate attire** based on context

### Troubleshooting Common Issues

**Audio Problems:**
- **Check device permissions** in browser/app
- **Test microphone** in settings before joining
- **Restart audio devices** if not detected
- **Update audio drivers** on desktop

**Video Issues:**
- **Camera permissions** must be granted
- **Close other applications** using camera
- **Sufficient bandwidth** for chosen quality
- **Browser hardware acceleration** enabled

**Connection Issues:**
- **Firewall settings** may block voice traffic
- **VPN interference** can cause quality issues
- **Network congestion** during peak hours
- **Mobile data limits** may affect quality
