# Account Setup

Setting up your HAOS account is quick and easy. Follow these steps to get started.

## ðŸ“ Creating Your Account

### Step 1: Visit the Sign-Up Page

1. Navigate to the HAOS website
2. Click **"Sign Up"** in the top-right corner
3. You'll be taken to the registration page

### Step 2: Choose Your Sign-Up Method

You can create an account using:

- **Email Address** (Recommended)
- **Google Account**
- **GitHub Account**

### Step 3: Complete Registration

**For Email Sign-Up:**
1. Enter your email address
2. Create a secure password (8+ characters)
3. Choose a unique username
4. Click **"Create Account"**

**For Social Sign-Up:**
1. Click your preferred provider (Google/GitHub)
2. Authorize HAOS to access your account
3. Choose a username (or use your existing one)
4. Complete the setup

## ðŸ‘¤ Completing Your Profile

### Setting Your Profile Picture

1. Click your avatar in the bottom-left corner
2. Select **"User Settings"**
3. Click **"Upload Avatar"**
4. Choose an image file (PNG, JPG, GIF supported)
5. Crop and save your image

### Customizing Your Profile

- **Display Name**: How others see you in servers
- **About Me**: A short bio (optional)
- **Status**: Show what you're doing
- **Theme**: Choose light or dark mode

## ðŸ” Security Settings

### Two-Factor Authentication (Recommended)

1. Go to **User Settings** > **Privacy & Security**
2. Click **"Enable Two-Factor Auth"**
3. Scan the QR code with your authenticator app
4. Enter the verification code
5. Save your backup codes in a safe place

### Privacy Settings

- **Who can send you friend requests**
- **Who can add you to servers**
- **Message notifications**
- **Read receipts**

## â“ Common Questions

**Q: Can I change my username later?**
A: Yes! You can change your display name anytime, but your unique username can only be changed once every 30 days.

**Q: What if I forget my password?**
A: Use the "Forgot Password" link on the sign-in page to reset it via email.

**Q: Can I delete my account?**
A: Yes, you can delete your account through User Settings > Privacy & Security > Delete Account. This action is permanent.
